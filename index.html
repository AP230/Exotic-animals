My Exotic animals

Axolotl (Amphibian)

As legend has it, the axolotl is the Aztec god of fire and lightning, Xolotl, which disguised himself as a salamander to avoid being sacrificed

Axolotl gills appear as feathery appendages on the outside of their bodies

Remarkably, axolotls are only found in a single location on Earth

Axolotls vary in colour from brown to pink to green.
